About

Pizza basically involves a crust usually made of flour, eggs, salt, and other flavoring, tomato sauce or pesto sauce, mozzarella cheese, and toppings. Toppings can be quite simple such as basil leaves or quite complicated such as pineapples, sausages, and even kabob meat. You can have a quite healthy pizza or a quite high calorie pizza depends on what type of crust or toppings you choose. The Oxford dictionary defines pizza as: noun.



A dish of Italian origin, consisting of a flat round base of dough baked with a topping of tomatoes and cheese, typically with added meat, fish, or vegetables: ‘a cheese and tomato pizza’ [mass noun] ‘a slice of pizza’ [as modifier] ‘a pizza restaurant’ Another take on Pizza would be: Possibly one of the easiest and tastiest forms of take-out to eat. Depending on the toppings you choose and what other ingredients are used to make it, Pizza can be a simple, healthy meal.
